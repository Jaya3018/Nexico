DuPont ROE Decomposition

ROE = Net Margin × Asset Turnover × Equity Multiplier. This breaks return on equity into why it moved — margin-driven, efficiency-driven, or leverage-driven — rather than reporting a single number.

Data Sources

Sample company figures (Infosys, TCS, Reliance Industries) are consolidated, in ₹ Crores, for FY2021–FY2025, sourced from public financial statements as reported on screener.in. Live market data (price, market cap, P/E) is pulled from Yahoo Finance at the moment you click "Fetch."
